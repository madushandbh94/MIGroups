MI Group is a dynamic and versatile business network that operates across two primary sectors: travel services and technical solutions. With a commitment to reliability, customer satisfaction, and innovation, MI Group delivers high-quality services to meet the diverse needs of its customers.

Key Divisions of MI Group:

MI Tours

MI Tours specializes in providing professional cab services throughout Sri Lanka. Its core values center on safety, honesty, and efficiency, ensuring that customers experience smooth and hassle-free travel at affordable rates. Key features include:

  • Safe and Reliable Transport: Expert drivers and well-maintained vehicles ensure a safe journey.
  • Affordable Pricing: Transparent and reasonable rates to suit various budgets.
  • Quick and Convenient Service: Timely pickups and drops for local and long-distance travel.

MI Tours positions itself as a trusted "Traveling Partner" for both tourists and local commuters, offering flexible and customer-friendly travel solutions.

Dhanu Tech

Dhanu Tech is the technology-focused branch of MI Group, providing cutting-edge technical solutions for home and business needs. Their services include:

  • Mobile Phone Repair and Accessories: Expert repairs and high-quality accessories for various mobile devices.
  • CCTV Systems: Installation, maintenance, and repair of CCTV cameras for enhanced security.
  • Smart Home Systems: Modern solutions to automate and streamline home functions.
  • Alarm Systems and Sensors: Advanced alarm and sensor setups to safeguard properties.

Dhanu Tech ensures that customers receive top-tier products and services tailored to their specific technical needs.

Core Values

  • Honesty and Integrity: Maintaining transparency in all dealings
  • Quality Service: Ensuring high standards in every project undertaken.
  • Innovation: Leveraging technology to offer modern solutions.

MI Group strives to build a reputation as a multi-industry leader, catering to a wide audience with diverse needs.
